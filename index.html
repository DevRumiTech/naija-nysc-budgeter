Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is NaijaNYSCBudgeter?

A free budgeting tool designed for Nigerian NYSC corps members to estimate allowance, expenses, and potential savings.

Is this tool affiliated with NYSC?

No. This tool is independent and not officially affiliated with NYSC.

How accurate are the results?

Results are estimates based on your inputs. Actual figures may vary.

Can I add my own expenses?

Yes. You can add, rename, or remove any expense category you want.

What if my state is not listed?

You can manually add your state using the custom state input.

Do you collect my data?

No. All calculations run locally in your browser. No data is stored.

Is the app free?

Yes. Ads may appear to support maintenance and improvements.
